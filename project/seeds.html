Seeds and Starts

There are many different seeds that grow well in my climate. The ones I plant from seed are acorn squash, Armenian Cucumber, Bush Beans, Butternut Squash, Cabbage, Carrot, Cucumber, Dark Red Beets, Radishes, Red Beets, Straightneck Squash, Sugar Snap Peas, Turnips, and Zucchini

Others that I plant in my garden are Tomatoes, Bell Peppers, and hot Peppers. However, I buy starts to plant those and Onions I plant from bulbs.

On the back of the seed packs it will tell you the time of year to plant, days to germinate, how deep to plant the seeds, how far apart to plant the seeds, and how long they take to grow.
